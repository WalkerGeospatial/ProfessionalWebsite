About Me

Hello! My name is Nathan Walker. I am an early-career GIS Developer at Ruekert & Mielke Inc, owner and founder of Magna Carto, LLC, and an aspiring spatial data scientist! I received a Master's of Science in Geography from Southern Illinois University - Edwardsville, and undergraduate degrees in Geography and Economics from University of Wisconsin - Eau Claire.

I fell in love with GIS and working with data early in my academic journey, and learned how to code to wrangle data, build models, and analyze insights with a variety of spatial and economic data. Along the way I discovered a knack for programming that led me to my developer role today. I have particular interests in AI, machine learning, spatial econometrics models, and a few other odds and ends you will find in my portfolio.

I pride myself on going beyond conventional tools — always exploring new technologies and methods that expand my capabilities and make the work more meaningful.

Business
Magna Carto, LLC
3D printed topographic map magnets of states and counties

Magna Carto is my 3D printing company for producing magnetic topographic maps. I produce a range of products, with the most popular being puzzle maps of counties that fit together. Other sizes of individual states and counties are also available.

I also produce custom maps and colors available on request for various organizations, using tools I developed to make models more reliably than other competitors.

R — Spatial Econometrics
Spatial Regression: Illinois Census Tracts
Compares OLS, Spatial Lag, and Spatial Error models predicting poverty and educational attainment across Illinois census tracts. Builds queen-contiguity spatial weights, runs Moran’s I and Lagrange Multiplier diagnostics to select the appropriate model specification, and exports formatted comparison tables via stargazer.

R — Data Analysis
Descriptive Statistics & Index Construction
Generates summary statistics (mean, median, IQR, skewness, kurtosis) and distribution plots for demographic and income variables across Illinois census tracts. Constructs a custom Educational Index via z-score normalization across four education attainment variables, and a Diversity Index based on deviation from proportional racial equality.

R — Statistics
Correlation Analysis & Matrix Visualization
Implements a reusable correlation_tests() function that runs Pearson correlation tests against a target field across any set of variables, returning coefficients, R², and p-values. Applied across GSS 2018 and U.S. States datasets to examine relationships between age, education, crime, and income variables. Produces correlation matrix plots with significance markers via corrplot.

Topographic Background Renderer
Procedurally generates an animated topographic contour line background using a Gaussian height field and Marching Squares. Peaks are randomized on each load, contours are extracted at multiple elevation levels, raw segments are chained into polylines, and each line draws itself in with a staggered ease-out animation.
